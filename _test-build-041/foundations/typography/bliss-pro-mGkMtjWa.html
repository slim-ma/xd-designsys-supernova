Typography

Moody’s main typeface is Bliss Pro for digital usage.

  • Bliss Pro
  • Typography for Data

Bliss Pro

Bliss Pro is a humanist sans typeface with a variety of sizes chosen for its high legibility and character sets for multiple languages.

We use Bliss Pro fonts in the following weights:

  • Bliss Pro Regular (400)
  • Bliss Pro Italic (400)
  • Bliss Pro Bold (700)
  • Bliss Pro Bold Italic (700)
  • Bliss Pro Extra Bold (800)
  • Bliss Pro Extra Bold Italic (800)

Specs

fontFamily: “Bliss Pro”, “Helvetica”, “arial”, san-serif

Establishing Hierarchy

When using Bliss Pro for Moody’s design, it’s important to establish hierarchy for better legibility by pairing text styes like a heading or subtitle with a body style.

Text on Backgrounds

Applying a background color is a simple way of establishing hierarchy and emphasis for layouts. Bliss Pro can be read on both light and dark backgrounds.

 

Text colors on backgrounds should score a WCAG 2.1 AA and AAA rating to meet accessibility compliance for legibility. See more details in Accessible Colors.
