How to enable the M’ Core Libraries

Follow the steps below to enable the M' Core Libraries in your Figma files.

View in Figma

 

 

Step 1

Within a Figma file, go to the Assets panel and click the Library icon.

Step 2

In the Libraries window, click the Central Product Group (CPG) tab and toggle on to enable M' Core Brand Library and M’ Core Icons libraries.

Step 3

In your Figma file, go to the Assets panel and expand the M' Core Brand Library or the M’ Core Icons to drag & drop components helpful for your project.
